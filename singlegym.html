Sports Direct Fitness Manchester is conveniently located in the city centre, on King Street. Inside the club you won’t feel like you’re in the middle of a city at all – there is plenty of space and equipment allowing you to work out at your own pace. You’ll find an excellent range of facilities at this club, as you’d expect from one of the UK’s leading networks of health clubs.


There is an excellent cardio area and resistance area at this club. The refurbished free weights area is now very spacious – it houses a good selection of weights, racks and benches and you won’t feel cramped while using any of these, even during the busiest periods. The new functional training area is a welcome addition to the facilities, allowing you to get a full workout with every visit.


The changing rooms have also benefitted from a recent makeover, providing you with everything that you need to get ready for the day after an early morning workout. Open between 6:30 AM and 10PM during the week, Sports Direct Manchester is perfect for those who work or live in and around central Manchester.
